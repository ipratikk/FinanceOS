# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone participating in FinanceOS and its community will be treated with respect and dignity, regardless of age, body size, caste, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, sexual identity, or sexual orientation.

We are dedicated to providing a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of these or other personal characteristics.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- **Be respectful** — Value each person's ideas, style, and perspective
- **Be direct but professional** — We are all professionals, and professionalism is handled differently across cultures
- **Be inclusive** — Seek diverse perspectives; remember that we learn the most from experience different from our own
- **Appreciate and accommodate differences** — Our differences make us stronger
- **Lead by example** — Model the behavior we want to see and match the effort we ask of others
- **Focus on what is best** for the community
- **Show empathy** towards other community members
- **Be patient** — Remember that people have different communication styles and that not everyone is using their native language

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment or bullying (online or offline)
- Deliberate intimidation
- Sexist, racist, homophobic, transphobic, or otherwise discriminatory jokes and language
- Unwanted sexual attention or advances
- Unwelcome physical contact
- Sustained disruption of talks, events, or discussions
- Other conduct that is inappropriate for a professional audience including people of many different backgrounds

## Enforcement

Community members who exhibit unacceptable behavior will be asked to stop and may be temporarily banned from participation. Repeated violations may result in permanent removal from the community.

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project maintainer at [ipratikgoel@gmail.com](mailto:ipratikgoel@gmail.com). All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All community spaces associated with FinanceOS
- Interactions related to FinanceOS in other community spaces
- When an individual is acting on behalf of FinanceOS
